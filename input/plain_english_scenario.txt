Test the shopping cart functionality:

1. Navigate to the product listing page
2. Search for "laptop" in the search box
3. Click on the first product in the results
4. Verify the product details page is displayed
5. Click the "Add to Cart" button
6. Verify the cart icon shows "1" item
7. Click on the cart icon
8. Verify the cart page shows the added product
9. Verify the product name matches what was added
10. Verify the quantity is 1
11. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
12. Verify the checkout page is displayed
